Creative direction

No Bullsh*t Fitness

A vibrant image for a fitness influencer

  • Creative direction
  • Branding
  • Wireframing
  • Website design
  • Site build

Lucy Mountain is a top Instagram influencer in the UK with over 300k followers. She describes herself as passive-aggressively calling out fitness bullsh*t. Working with the incredible Genup Digital I designed her new identity and membership website.

Brief

Create a vibrant, fun, tongue and cheek brand that represents what Lucy is doing. Design and build the Nobs membership website.

Aims

A look that is modern, but differentiates Lucy from the rest of the fitness experts. Approachable, friendly and not too serious.

Homepage

Together with the website animations, fun colour scheme, layout and language used the site is both modern and friendly.

Simple and clean

Using space, shapes, colour and clean typography the site is elegant in its simplicity.

Membership

The membership website allows users to get exclusive content, clear actions around the site drive users to sign up.

Videos

An exclusive aspect to the membership site are videos. They include nutrition advice and training guides. The index page makes it easy to find what you are looking for.

Video

The video template has a clear action to play the video on first view. Users can also read about what is included in the video and save it for later.

Contact

Lucy is a fun, outgoing lady who also provides one on one training. The contact page allows potential customers to get in touch.

Fully responsive

The site is fully responsive, creating a seamless experience on all devices.
